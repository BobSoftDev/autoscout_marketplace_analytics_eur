# Governance Operating Model

## Roles
| Role | Responsibility |
|------|----------------|
| Data Engineering | Bronze & Silver pipelines |
| Analytics Engineering | Gold KPIs |
| BI Owners | Tableau & Streamlit |
| Finance | Revenue definitions |
| Executive | KPI usage |

## Controls
- Gold tables are the only source of truth
- KPIs may not be redefined in BI tools
- MoM & YoY are locked after month-end

## Cadence
- Weekly KPI review
- Monthly executive performance review
- Quarterly metric audit
